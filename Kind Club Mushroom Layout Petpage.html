Quirky Aesthetic Kind Club

In place of the Gift Exchange many of you have been familiar with in past years at Quirky Aesthetic, we are now offering the 'Quirky Kind Club'! A club for this activity will offer a type of experience to these participants allowing members interact on a more personal level and learn more about the interests of their fellow club members; forming a small community from within of those that enjoy spreading kindness, friendship and most of all ... presents!

about us

Members participating in this club will send presents to all other club members once per month! There will be no price cap for this activity and members will be encouraged to send gifts as often as they like; however we will send out our newsletter for this club once per month with all club members listed for the official exchange of each month. Once we have a few or more club members, we could potentially group them together in the directory for easy reference, offer a neoboard for club members, have club auctions and many other fun perks. If you're interested in holding a leadership position within this club, please contact katelynnfitz.
